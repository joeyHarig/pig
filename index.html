pig | the game

Pig is a dice game, played in turns. You roll the pair of dice as many times as you would like during your turn. Each roll is added to your Current Score.


If you roll a 1 on either die, your Current Score will set back to 0 and your turn will be over.


You may choose to Hold and by doing so, add your Current Score to your Total Score and pass the turn.


A player wins when their Total score reaches 100.
